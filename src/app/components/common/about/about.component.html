About Us

Sofia Knights is an american football club in Sofia, Bulgaria. We are founded in 2018.

Currently we are the champions of Bulgaria. Holding the title undefeated last season.

We also play international friendly matches. Our future goal is to stick up a good squad and play high european level of football. We want to make Bulgaria one of the best countries in Europe in terms of american football.

Vertues

Since the founding of the club, we the members of Sofia Knights are proud to compete, teach and live following these vertues:

Honor

Always say the truth. No cheater can truly win

Purity

Live with a clean sight and good intentions

Integrity

Be objective, just and persistent in your actions

Courage

Do not doubt. Hesitation is your worst enemy
